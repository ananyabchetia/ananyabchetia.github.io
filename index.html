I interview my sources in Assamese, Hindi and English.

About Me

I'm a data journalist from Assam, India who was raised in Saudi Arabia. I cover education policies, climate change and information warfare with a data driven lens. That includes scraping ugly PDFs and messy websites, creating interactive charts and maps on QGIS and successfully filing FOIA requests. My stories have been recognized by the Society of Professional Journalists' Mark of Excellence Awards and Virginia's press conferences for Best In-Depth News Story. I also won my university's Global Engagement Award and the Student Journalist of the Year award for my role as executive editor of the student paper, The Collegian. Outside of reporting, I'm curled up with a juicy novel, drawing on my ipad (like the potrait here) or curating the perfect spotify playlist.
